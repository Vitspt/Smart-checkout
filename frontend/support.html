❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I scan a product? expand_more
Go to the Scanner page using the bottom navigation or the floating scan button. You can either use your camera to scan a barcode or enter the barcode ID manually (e.g., 1001, 2003). The product will be automatically added to your cart.
What barcodes can I use? expand_more
For testing, use these barcode IDs: 1001 (Rice), 1005 (Amul Ghee), 2003 (Cadbury Dairy Milk), 3001 (Bisleri Water), 3003 (Coca-Cola), 4001 (Dove Soap), 4003 (Colgate), 4005 (Dettol Handwash).
How does payment work? expand_more
After reviewing your cart, proceed to Payment. Select your preferred method (UPI, Card, or Wallet) and click "Pay Now". The payment is simulated — a success screen will appear and a QR receipt will be generated.
What is the QR code for? expand_more
After successful payment, a QR code is generated containing your order details. Show this QR at the exit gate for verification. The security staff will scan it to confirm your payment status.
Can I add items from multiple categories? expand_more
Yes! You can add items from Grocery, Snacks, Drinks, and Personal Care to the same cart. All items are stored in your device and the total is calculated automatically.
Is there delivery or shipping? expand_more
No. SmartCheckout is an IN-STORE system only. You physically pick items from the shelves, scan them, pay digitally, and exit after verification. There is no delivery or shipping.
